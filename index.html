The Lab at GenerTech

creating tomorrow


GenerTech is changing the way we use electronics.

We spent years pursuing our goal of developing the worlds first true artificial intelligence. After reaching that goal, we used this new technology to revolutionize fields including healthcare, transportation, computer science, and digital imaging. But that was only step one.


Smart products have long been an industry dream. Previous attempts at these products were smart, but they were not intelligent. GenerTech Labs has begun testing intelligent products, with the hopes of bringing these devices to market. Conscious, perceptive, and always learning, they are unlike anything you've ever seen before.

We Dream of Systems That Can Regulate Themselves



Technology is not just a collection of electronic devices connected to the internet. The past 50 years have seen us bear witness to more than the rapid integration of computers. We are witnessing a fundamental change to the ecology of the world.


Creating devices that can think and act independently means creating a new species. We are creating a new techno-ecology. This is evolution as a universal principle, applied to electronic objects.


Questions abound. How will this techno-ecology meld with our own? Can these systems organize themselves? Do we want them to?


GenerTech aims to explore the possibilities of this new world.
